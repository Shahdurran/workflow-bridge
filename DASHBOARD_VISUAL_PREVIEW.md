## Typography Scale

### Headers
- **h1**: 2.25rem (36px) - Page titles
- **h2**: 1.5rem (24px) - Section headers
- **h3**: 1.25rem (20px) - Card titles
- **h4**: 1rem (16px) - Workflow names

### Body Text
- **Large**: 1.125rem (18px) - Stats
- **Base**: 1rem (16px) - Body copy
- **Small**: 0.875rem (14px) - Labels
- **XSmall**: 0.75rem (12px) - Captions

### Weights
- **Bold (700)**: Headers, important numbers
- **Semibold (600)**: Subheaders, buttons
- **Medium (500)**: Navigation links
- **Regular (400)**: Body text
